### Pin Mapping
stm32f4xx_hal::i2s::I2s pins -> Amp Pins:

SPI::Ws -> LRC
SPI::Ck -> BCLK
SPI::Mck -> Not Needed ( ->  stm32f4xx_hal::i2s::NoMasterClock verwenden?)
SP::Sd -> DIN
